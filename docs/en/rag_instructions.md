# 🧠 User Guide: Investor Knowledge Base (RAG)
 
This feature allows the AI to not only analyze numbers but also follow **your specific investment philosophy**. The system uses a technology called RAG (*Retrieval-Augmented Generation*) to "read" your documents before writing each research report.
 
## 🎯 Objective
The primary goal is to **personalize risk criteria**. While the AI can analyze the market, it doesn't know which risks personally concern you or what your work ethics are until you explain them.
 
## 🛠️ How to use it
You can upload PDF files (books, notes, methodologies) or create a simple text document with your rules.
 
### Example 1: The Ethical "Blacklist"
If you want the AI to automatically discard certain companies, upload a file that says:
> "ETHICAL CRITERIA: I do not want to invest under any circumstances in companies related to weapons, mass surveillance, or personal data exploitation. Examples: PALANTIR, META, LOCKHEED MARTIN."
 
### Example 2: Risk Management Rules
> "GOLDEN RULE: Never accept a trade if the company reports earnings in less than 48 hours, as volatility is unpredictable."
 
### Example 3: Book Methodology
You can upload classic trading books. The AI will extract advice from them:
> "According to the Stan Weinstein method, we only buy if the price is above the 30-week moving average."
 
## 🚀 What happens in the Report?
When the AI generates a report for a detected opportunity:
1. It will search your "Knowledge Base" for any rules affecting that symbol or sector.
2. If it finds an ethical prohibition, the **Fundamental Verdict** will be **DISCARDED**.
3. If it finds risk advice, it will include it in the **Risk Factors** section.
 
---
*Note: You can update this knowledge at any time by uploading new files in the '🧠 Investor Knowledge Base' tab.*
